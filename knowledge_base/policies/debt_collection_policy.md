# Debt Collection Complaint Guidance

Debt collection complaints may involve attempts to collect a debt not owed, communication frequency, harassment, threats, incorrect balance, lack of debt validation, or disputes about ownership of the debt.

Analysts should verify account ownership, debt status, communication history, and validation documentation. Complaints alleging harassment, threats, or legal action should be escalated. Customer responses should be empathetic, factual, and should not confirm debt responsibility without verification.
