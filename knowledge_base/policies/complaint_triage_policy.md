# Complaint Triage Policy

All customer complaints must be classified by product, issue type, risk level, and required next action. Complaints involving fraud, identity theft, credit reporting harm, mortgage foreclosure, debt collection harassment, legal threats, or vulnerable customers must be escalated for human review.

Low-risk complaints can receive a standard acknowledgement and be routed to the correct service team. Medium-risk complaints require analyst review within the standard SLA. High-risk and critical complaints require urgent review, documentation of evidence, and escalation to a specialized team.

The response must acknowledge the customer's concern, avoid admitting liability, avoid promising a specific outcome, and explain the next review step.
