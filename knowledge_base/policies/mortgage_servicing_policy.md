# Mortgage Servicing Complaint Guidance

Mortgage servicing complaints can involve payment application, escrow, fees, loan modification, foreclosure, or servicing transfer issues. These cases can create significant financial harm and reputational risk.

Complaints mentioning foreclosure, eviction, payment misapplication, or failed loss-mitigation review should be treated as high or critical priority. The analyst should route the complaint to mortgage servicing specialists and document the timeline of customer contact, payment history, and pending actions.
