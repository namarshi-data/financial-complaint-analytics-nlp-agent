# Fraud and Identity Theft Escalation Policy

Complaints involving suspected fraud, unauthorized transactions, account takeover, identity theft, or forged applications require high-priority handling. The case should be escalated to fraud operations and may require temporary account controls, transaction review, customer authentication, and documentation collection.

The response should acknowledge urgency, provide a safe next step, and avoid requesting sensitive personal information through unsecured channels. If a customer reports active harm, the agent should recommend immediate human review.
